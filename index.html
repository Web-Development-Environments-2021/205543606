About Me



History

I was born on 9 September 1939 in Jerusalem during the Mandate era to Rachel "Ray" and Yosef Yoel Rivlin.
I am an israeli politician and lawyer. I am the current israeli president, since 2014.

Prior to that, as a member of the "Likud" party, I've been the Master of Communication and Speaker of the Knesset.



Fun facts

  • I am fluent in Arabic.
  • I am a vegetrian.
  • I am a huge fan of Beitar Yerushalayim.
  • I like to start every morning with 3 kilometers walk on Jerusalem's Forest.
